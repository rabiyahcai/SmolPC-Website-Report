SmolPC

Abstract

The Problem

Artificial intelligence is rapidly transforming education, yet access to AI-powered learning tools remains deeply unequal. Leading solutions such as Microsoft Copilot and Google AI require costly subscriptions and reliable internet connectivity, placing them out of reach for the millions of students attending under-resourced schools worldwide. As a result, the students who stand to benefit most from AI-assisted learning are precisely those who are locked out of it. Meanwhile, teachers in these schools continue to spend excessive time manually producing lesson materials, slides, and visual aids, with little technological support to help them meet the diverse needs of their classrooms.

Our Solution

SmolPC is a free, open-source, fully offline AI assistant suite designed to run on low-cost devices such as Intel N200 laptops. Our AI assistant is integrated directly into LibreOffice, CodeHelper, GIMP, and Blender, enabling students and teachers to control educational software through natural language without requiring any internet connection. Powered by locally deployed large language models via OpenVino, SmolPC generates slides and documents, guides students through programming concepts and debugging, and teaches image editing and 3D modelling through interactive AI assistance. SmolPC ensures complete data privacy, making it a safe and practical solution for real classroom deployment.

Our Impact: Education for All

SmolPC demonstrates that powerful AI-assisted education does not have to come with a price tag. Following consultation with teachers through our supervisor, Professor Mohamedally, and a demonstration to the National Autistic Society, feedback has been overwhelmingly positive, with educators expressing strong interest in adopting SmolPC in their classrooms. SmolPC was also showcased at the BETT Conference, one of the world's leading educational technology events, where it was met with enthusiasm from educators and institutions alike. We hope to see SmolPC deployed in schools globally, giving every student access to an AI learning assistant that supports them across writing, coding, design, and beyond.
